6. the longtermer's compounding playbook — how a year here compounds
+ bonus: a 15-min voice-memo q&a, and my direct line for one real question

**price:** $49 one-time. yours forever, updated as ns evolves.
**price logic:** the flight costs 20x this. one wasted week here costs more.
this is the cheapest de-risking of the biggest move you'll make this year.
**founding price:** early buyers lock $49 and get every future update free.
it goes up as it gets more complete.

**guarantee (risk reversal):** read it. if it's not the single most useful
thing you read before moving, email me — full refund, keep the guide.

**the referral judo:** apply through ns.com/adam/apply and the guide is free.
so the guide sells on its own AND turns buyers into referrals.
